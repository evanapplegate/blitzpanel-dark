FAQ

What is Blitzpanel?

Blitzpanel is a design-to-factory platform for industrial control panels. Engineers can design online, get instant quotes, and have assemblies built automatically.

Who is Blitzpanel for?

Electrical engineers, mechanical engineers, machine builders, and companies that need UL508A-style control panels, DIN-rail assemblies, and industrial enclosures.

How is Blitzpanel different from a traditional panel shop?

Traditional shops require back-and-forth quoting and custom drawings. Blitzpanel automates design rules, quoting, and manufacturing, cutting lead times from weeks to days.

What kinds of components are available?

Our library includes breakers, relays, drives, power supplies, PLCs, terminals, wire, connectors, and more.

Do you ship panels fully assembled?

Yes. Panels are built, wired, tested, and shipped ready to install.

Can Blitzpanel handle compliance like UL508A?

Yes. Our system enforces UL508A design rules and labeling during the design process.

Do I have to use the design tool?

No. If you already have a design or just need a quote, you can upload your files or request a quote directly. The design tool is optional and only a means to further streamline the ordering process.
